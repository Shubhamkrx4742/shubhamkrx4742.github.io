About PaperBill

PaperBill was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to provide small businesses in India with affordable, easy-to-use accounting software that works both offline and online.

We understand the challenges faced by small business owners - unreliable internet connections, complex software, and the need for GST compliance. That's why we built PaperBill to work seamlessly offline while offering the convenience of cloud features when you need them.

Today, PaperBill is trusted by over 50,000 small businesses across India to manage their invoicing, inventory, and accounting needs.

Our team is based in Bangalore and we're committed to continuous improvement based on customer feedback. We release regular updates with new features and improvements to make PaperBill even better.
